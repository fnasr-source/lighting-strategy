# Simulations Labs — Version Comparison

## Three Versions Produced

| Dimension | V1: Executive Light | V2: Balanced (Main) | V3: Deep Dive |
|-----------|-------------------|---------------------|---------------|
| **Path** | `v1-executive-light/` | `index.html` (root) | `v3-deep-dive/` |
| **Audience** | Board / C-level | Operations + Strategy | Analyst / Technical |
| **Sections** | KPIs, Accomplishments, Blockers, Next Steps | All 6 sections + charts | Extended tables, full meeting index, methodology |
| **Read time** | ~2 min | ~8 min | ~15 min |
| **Charts** | None (KPI cards only) | 8 interactive charts + risk matrix | Data tables (no visualizations) |
| **Data depth** | Summary aggregates | Aggregates + breakdowns | Per-record detail |
| **Methodology** | Not included | Brief appendix | Full methodology section |

## Strengths & Weaknesses

### V1: Executive Light
- ✅ Quick to scan, decision-focused
- ✅ Highlights blockers and actions clearly
- ❌ No evidence trail for claims
- ❌ Not suitable for operational planning

### V2: Balanced (Recommended Default)
- ✅ Best balance of strategy + evidence
- ✅ Interactive charts with source traceability
- ✅ Risk matrix and 30/60/90 plan included
- ✅ Confidence badges on every section
- ❌ May be too detailed for a quick board glance

### V3: Deep Dive
- ✅ Complete data audit trail
- ✅ Full meeting index with recording IDs
- ✅ Task-level operational detail
- ✅ Full methodology documentation
- ❌ Too dense for non-technical audience
- ❌ No visual charts (tables only)

## Recommendation

**Primary share link: V2 Balanced** (`index.html`)

Use V1 for quick stakeholder updates. Use V3 for internal team reference and audit trail.
