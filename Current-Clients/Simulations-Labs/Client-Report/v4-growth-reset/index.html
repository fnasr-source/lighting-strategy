Current situation

Where the account stands now

The current problem is not a lack of activity. It is a lack of conversion quality. The reset is designed to solve that by narrowing the wedge, tightening the offer, and forcing follow-through on the existing warm opportunities.

Executive diagnosis
Problem is conversion, not reach alone
  • The previous motion created activity, but not enough downstream movement.
  • The message was too broad across too many personas and use cases at once.
  • The funnel had weak proof and no hard operational discipline inside the CRM.
  • The reset changes the KPI from “more outbound” to “more booked demos and qualified conversations.”

The wedge is now education-first: institutions, instructors, and training teams that need measurable hands-on cyber learning without infrastructure overhead.
