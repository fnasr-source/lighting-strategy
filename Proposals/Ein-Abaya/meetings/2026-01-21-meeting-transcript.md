## Key Points

### Current channel performance
- Instagram is currently stronger than TikTok.
- TikTok is active and still interesting, but performance is not as reliable as Instagram.
- Snapchat was tested and did not perform strongly.
- Google activity exists in some form, including shopping / YouTube / remarketing references, but the actual role of Google still needed clarification.

### Website and customer experience
- The store exists, but the team is already considering a stronger online-store experience.
- The website needs stronger product presentation, lookbook treatment, more natural imagery, and visible reviews.
- Fouad and Ahmad stressed that ads and landing experience must reinforce each other.
- The site should create an experience that helps a new visitor trust the brand quickly.

### Brand positioning
- Awatif clarified that **Ein Abaya** is a broader, more accessible premium brand.
- **Maison Ein** is the higher-end line with stronger quality and higher price points.
- The elevated line is intended to benefit from trust already built by Ein Abaya.
- Dresses are part of the brand’s expansion direction.

### Lifecycle and retention observations
- Fouad described the work as optimization across different stages:
  - awareness
  - purchase
  - repurchase
  - reviews / social proof
- WhatsApp exists in the flow, but the welcome offer is delayed due to platform limitations.
- The team discussed faster alternatives through email or better-triggered automation.

### What the client wanted next
- A deeper follow-up session with the marketing team present.
- More detail on what could be improved across ads, attribution, customer experience, and conversion.
