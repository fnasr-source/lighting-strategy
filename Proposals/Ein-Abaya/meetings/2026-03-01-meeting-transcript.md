- The client was evaluating platform options and was not yet locked into one during this meeting.

- Zid was also discussed as a viable option.

### Marketing plan explanation
- Ahmad described the 90-day marketing plan as a roadmap from current state to target state.
- The plan would map:
  - short-term goals
  - long-term goals
  - the steps required to move from current sales level to future target

- Ahmad asked for directional data such as acquisition cost, conversion rate, and budget context.

- An NDA was mentioned as an option if deeper data sharing became necessary.

### Commercial framing discussed

- There was also discussion of future paid-ads continuation after the initial setup period.
