## 1. Competitive Landscape Overview

The Egyptian luxury interior design market features a growing roster of established and emerging firms, particularly clustered around **New Cairo's 5th Settlement and Katameya**. The market is driven by massive compound developments (SODIC, Palm Hills, Mountain View, Ora Developers) and the New Administrative Capital.

Despite the market's growth, **no single firm dominates digitally**. Most rely on word-of-mouth and Instagram portfolios, creating an opportunity for MHK to establish digital-first market leadership.
