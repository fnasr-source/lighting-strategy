Section 01

Competitive Landscape Overview

The Egyptian luxury interior design market features a growing roster of established and emerging firms, particularly clustered around New Cairo's 5th Settlement and Katameya. The market is driven by compound developments (SODIC, Palm Hills, Mountain View) and the New Administrative Capital.

MHK has arguably the strongest service offering (full turnkey + 3 countries) but the weakest digital presence among direct competitors.
This is both the biggest risk and the biggest opportunity.
