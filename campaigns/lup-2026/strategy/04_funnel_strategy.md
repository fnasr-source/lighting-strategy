# Funnel Strategy — Leading Under Pressure 2026

---

## Funnel Overview

```
AWARENESS ──────── INTEREST ──────── DESIRE ──────── ACTION
  Cold Outreach       Landing Page       Nurture         Apply/
  LinkedIn DMs        Brochure DL        Sequences       Register
  Warm Intros         Private Brief      Social Proof
  Content Posts       Webinar Invite     FOMO/Urgency
```

---

## Stage 1: AWARENESS (Top of Funnel)

### Channel 1: Cold Email Outreach
- **Volume:** 600–900 total sends from ~920 targeted contacts (wave-based extraction)
- **Sequences:**
  - *Executive Sequence* (C-suite targets) — 4 emails over 12 days
  - *L&D/HR Sequence* (sponsors) — 4 emails over 12 days
  - *Warm Network Sequence* (connections) — 3 emails over 8 days
- **Goal:** Drive to landing page or request a private briefing
- **KPI:** 25%+ open rate, 5%+ reply rate, 3%+ landing page click

### Channel 2: LinkedIn Direct Outreach
- **Approach:** Manual, personalized connection requests + messages
- **Target:** ICP-matched profiles with visible trigger events
- **Volume:** 50–100 targeted connections
- **Message Framework:** Lead with empathy ("Saw you're navigating [trigger event]...") → Offer value → Invite to landing page
- **KPI:** 30%+ acceptance rate, 10%+ message reply rate

### Channel 3: Warm Network Activation
- **Approach:** Personal emails from Rania Abdalla and Robert Mazelanik
- **Target:** Existing contacts, past program alumni, referral partners
- **Volume:** 50–100 contacts
- **Message:** Personal recommendation with exclusive "priority seat" language
- **KPI:** 15%+ response rate, 5%+ application rate

### Channel 4: Content Marketing
- **LinkedIn posts** by Rania / Aspire HR about crisis leadership themes
- **Share articles/research** from Sameh Abadir's IMD publications
- **Joyce Azzam stories** about resilience and leading under literal pressure
- **Frequency:** 2–3 posts per week for 6 weeks pre-event

---

## Stage 2: INTEREST (Mid-Funnel)

### Landing Page
- **URL:** https://lup.admireworks.com
- **Structure:** Full StoryBrand framework (see brandscript)
- **Primary CTA:** "Reserve Your Seat" → Application form
- **Secondary CTA:** "Download Brochure" → PDF download + email capture
- **Tertiary CTA:** "Request a Private Briefing" → Calendar booking
- **KPI:** 8%+ conversion (brochure download), 3%+ application form submission

### Private Briefing Calls
- **Format:** 15-minute 1-on-1 call with Rania Abdalla or Robert Mazelanik
- **Target:** High-value prospects, group buyers, C-suite contacts
- **Content:** Personalized program overview, ROI discussion, Q&A, address objections
- **KPI:** 40%+ conversion from briefing to registration

|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0 | Welcome + Brochure delivery | Deliver value, set expectations |

- Speaker credential highlights
- Partner institution logos (IMD, Stanford, SRI)
- "As seen in" media mentions

## Stage 4: ACTION (Bottom of Funnel)

### Application Process
1. Prospect fills out application form on landing page (name, title, company, email, phone, brief motivation)
2. Aspire HR team reviews application within 24 hours
3. Confirmation email with payment details and logistics
4. Welcome package with pre-program assessment instructions

### Pricing & Incentives
| Tier | Price | Incentive |
|---|---|---|
| **Standard** | €4,900 | — |
| **Alumni** | €4,300 | 12% savings |
| **Group (3+)** | €4,300/pp | 12% per person + team assessment |
| **Early Bird** | Consider €4,500 | Applied 30+ days before event |

### Urgency & Scarcity Levers
- "Limited to 25 participants" — real scarcity based on cohort model
- Countdown timer on landing page (days until April 20)
- "X seats remaining" — update dynamically
- Early bird deadline — create time pressure
- "Applications close March 31" — hard deadline
