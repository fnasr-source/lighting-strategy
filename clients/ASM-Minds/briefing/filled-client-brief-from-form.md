## 2. Brand Background

### How did your company start? What is your mission?

`Confirmed`

ASM Minds started at the beginning of COVID as a student-led service initiative. The original idea was simple: divide course preparation across a large student group so each student would only need to master one lecture deeply per week, then re-organize and rewrite the material into a much better learning format for everyone else.

Within one month, the team had produced structured explanations for complex subjects across roughly 100 lectures. Over time, the founder expanded from collaborative lecture prep into writing medical books and references tailored to their faculty. As the founding student group graduated, the idea was converted into a for-profit company to scale the model properly.

The brand now describes itself as a leading medical education company with:

- more than 180 medical references and books under `ASM Minds Books`
- distribution of more than 150,000 books per year inside and outside Egypt
- two Alexandria branches operating daily from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM except Friday
- specialized medical courses
- a medical question-bank platform with monthly, quarterly, semiannual, and annual subscriptions under `Grade Gain`
- printed question-book products under the same `Grade Gain` line
- a `Gold edition` line of condensed review books
- `private tutoring`
- an e-books app with annotation and IP-protection features
- a paid courses app for more advanced recorded explanations
- ongoing free educational explanations by the student team
- free student follow-up and guidance through Telegram channels for each study year

Working mission implied by the form:

- simplify medical learning
- save students time
- improve academic performance
- provide a complete learning ecosystem rather than a single-format product
