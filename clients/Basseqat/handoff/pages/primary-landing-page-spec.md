# Primary Landing Page Spec

## Page Purpose
Convert qualified traffic from Meta Ads into WhatsApp conversation starts. The page must educate, build trust, and provide enough proof/process clarity to motivate a conversation — without hype or guaranteed return promises.

## Target Audience
Egyptian expats in Saudi Arabia (40+), cautious, skeptical, proof-seeking.

## Language
Egyptian Arabic (not MSA). Natural, warm, specific — not corporate or translated.

## Primary CTA
"ابدأ محادثة على الواتساب" (Start a WhatsApp conversation)
Click-to-WhatsApp button — present at minimum 3x on the page (hero, mid, bottom).

## Technical Requirements
- Mobile-first design (audience is primarily on mobile in KSA)
- RTL layout (Arabic)
- Fast loading (3G-friendly for expat network conditions)
- Meta Pixel installed for conversion tracking
- UTM parameter capture
- Click-to-WhatsApp with pre-filled message
- Schema markup for organization and FAQ

## Design Direction
- Clean, documentary feel — not flashy or sales-heavy
- Natural earth tones (greens, sand, warm neutrals)
- Real photography over stock imagery
- Readable Arabic typography (consider Noor or similar Arabic font from brand assets)
- Enough whitespace to feel calm and serious

**Source:** Direct-response strategy, copy frameworks, client brief
