## Key Insights Summary

### Client Decision Status
- **Client confirmed intention to proceed** with Admireworks — received internal approval
- Interested in **Option 3 — Full Growth Partnership** (funnel + optimization + ads management)
- Requested **more details on pricing** and comparison with agency alternatives
- Asked for an **irresistible package** — "اعمل باكيج مغرية" (make an offer we can't refuse)
- Client is comparing Admireworks media buying vs. a separate agency handling everything

### Media Buying Discussion
- Client is deciding between Admireworks for media buying vs. an external agency
- **Leaning toward Admireworks** if the financials and performance are better
- Key advantage: everything in one platform = faster decision-making, real-time insights
- No waiting for monthly agency reports — all data in one place
- Fouad clarified: strategy covers the entire sales funnel, not just a segment
- Channels: start with one channel in Month 1, expand to two in Months 2-3, then scale (Snapchat, multi-market)

### Reply Management (WhatsApp/Messenger)
- Client asked about the Reply Management add-on
- Previously discussed: Fouad's platform handles WhatsApp and Messenger replies
- **Fouad offered first 2 months free** to let the client experience the system
- Islam referenced Genioo.do pricing at ~6,000 EGP/mo for similar reply management
- Client wants to beat/match Genioo.do pricing

### CRM System (New — Not in Original Proposal)
- Khaled mentioned hearing about Fouad's **integrated CRM system** — "سمعت أخبار متداولة"
- **First time seeing the CRM** — Khaled explicitly noted this
- Fouad demonstrated the CRM capabilities:
  - **Lead assignment** — automatic flow assigns leads to sales team members
  - **Role-based access** — sales reps see only their own leads; managers see everything
  - **Lead source tracking** — UTM sources, campaign attribution, which video/post generated the lead
  - **Search** — by name, phone number, email, and other criteria
  - **Pipeline management** — deal stages, status tracking, done deals with amounts
  - **Task management** — assigned tasks per team member
  - **Activity tracking** — manager can track what each salesperson has done
  - **Flow automation** — when a lead enters, auto-assign + email + WhatsApp sequence
  - **Filtering** — filter leads by campaign, landing page, salesperson
  - **Sales reporting** — per-salesperson performance metrics
- Islam confirmed: leads from campaigns flow **directly into the CRM**
- Authority levels discussed: sales rep can only see/edit their own records; sales manager has oversight
- Khaled cautioned: needs proper access controls to prevent accidental data deletion
- Islam's pragmatic view: "If Fouad built it, it's integrated, let's use it — worst case we export to Excel"
- Client wants to evaluate CRM during the engagement period

### Creative / UGC Videos
- Client reviewed the **Creative Pack** (8 videos per quarter at 22,000 EGP)
- Discussed video types: mix of scripted, UGC-style, and AI-generated
- Khaled's vision for content:
  - **Founder-facing videos** — Khaled appears as the face of Basseqat, building trust
  - **Emotional/fear-based UGC** — actor-based scenarios (e.g., retirement fears, income diversification)
  - Two content directions: (1) authority/founder content, (2) relatable problem-scenario content
- Islam suggested starting with 1-2 UGC videos alongside the campaign
- Fouad: can include video in the campaign package; difference is between using existing content vs. producing new

### Business Context
- **No existing web presence** — zero followers, website under construction
- Starting from scratch with social media and digital marketing
- Company is in early stages of business operations
- Based in Egypt
- Timeline discussed: ~1 month from kickoff to initial results
  - 2 weeks: strategy finalization
  - 2 weeks: build and launch
- Client wants to start with landing page to generate leads, then expand

### Social Media Management
- Fouad clarified: social media management is **NOT his core service**
- Would manage it via a partner agency in the backend if needed
- Khaled agreed: better to keep Fouad focused on his core strengths (funnel + ads)
- Social media would be an overhead if handled through Fouad indirectly
