# Research Workspace

Use this folder for external market and competitor research before strategy synthesis.

## Purpose
- reduce generic strategy output
- separate client-provided truth from externally researched market context
- make strategic claims traceable
- force deeper thinking before copy and funnel design

## Minimum Expected Outputs
- research budget
- research report
- competitor scan
- customer language bank
- market and digital environment scan
- source log
- research review
- contamination checklist

Starter draft files:
- `research-budget.md`
- `research-requests.json`
- `work-order.md`
- `research-report.md`
- `competitor-scan.md`
- `customer-language-bank.md`
- `market-environment-scan.md`
- `source-log.md`
- `research-review.md`
- `CONTAMINATION_CHECKLIST.md`

Reference docs:
- `TOOLING_STACK.md`
- `RESEARCH_BUDGETING.md`
- `RESEARCH_RUNNERS.md`

## Rule
External research should inform strategy, not replace client truth.
Document sources and clearly separate:
- confirmed source-backed facts
- inference
- open questions
- copied assumptions or examples that were rejected or revalidated
