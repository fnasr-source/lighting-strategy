## 1. Pain Phrases (From Market Research + Brief)

### Financial Anxiety
- "أنا معايا فلوس دلوقتي بس لو فضلت أصرفها" — I have money now but if I keep spending it [from meeting transcript]
- "لما هيبقى عندي 60 سنة — هعمل ايه؟" — When I'm 60, what will I do? [from meeting transcript — Khaled's UGC idea]
- "الفلوس بتأكل نفسها" — The money eats itself (inflation concern — common Egyptian expat phrase)
- "بشتغل بره بس مش عارف أوفر إزاي" — Working abroad but don't know how to save properly
- "خايف أرجع مصر ومعييش حاجة" — Afraid to return to Egypt with nothing
- "كل يوم الجنيه بينزل" — Every day the pound drops

### Skepticism and Trust
- "شفنا كتير ناس اتنصبوا" — We've seen many people get scammed
- "مين يضمن لي؟" — Who guarantees me?
- "كلام حلو بس واقع ايه؟" — Nice words but what's the reality?
- "بيوعدوا بأرقام مستحيلة" — They promise impossible numbers
- "أنا مش هدفع حاجة قبل ما أفهم" — I won't pay anything before I understand
- "عايز أشوف بعينيا" — I want to see with my own eyes

### Decision Paralysis
- "مش عارف أختار ايه" — I don't know what to choose
- "كل حد بيقول كلام مختلف" — Everyone says different things
- "خايف أغلط" — Afraid to make a mistake
- "محتاج أفهم الحاجة كويس" — I need to understand this properly

## 2. Desire Phrases

### Financial Security
- "عايز حاجة تجيبلي دخل ثابت" — I want something that brings steady income
- "عايز أبني مستقبل لولادي" — I want to build a future for my kids
- "عايز فلوسي تشتغل بدل ما تقعد" — I want my money to work instead of sitting idle
- "حاجة مضمونة ومفيهاش مخاطرة كبيرة" — Something safe with low risk

### Connection to Egypt
- "عايز أمتلك حاجة في بلدي" — I want to own something in my homeland
- "لما أرجع يبقى عندي حاجة" — When I return, I'll have something
- "أرض في مصر أحسن من أي حاجة" — Land in Egypt is better than anything

### Trust and Process
- "ورّيني الأوراق" — Show me the documents
- "عايز أعرف الإجراءات خطوة خطوة" — I want to know the steps one by one
- "اللي يقول لي الحقيقة أنا معاه" — Whoever tells me the truth, I'm with them
- "عايز حد يفهمني مش حد يبيعلي" — I want someone to help me understand, not someone selling me

## 3. Objection Phrases

### Trust-Related
- "إيه الضمان؟" — What's the guarantee?
- "ممكن تاخدوا فلوسي وتمشوا" — You might take my money and leave
- "ازاي أتأكد؟" — How do I make sure?
- "فيه ناس اتحرقت في حاجات زي كده" — People got burned in things like this

### Financial
- "مش هينفع أدفع ده كله مرة واحدة" — Can't pay all at once
- "لو معملتش فلوس هرجعهم؟" — If I don't make money, will I get it back?
- "ليه أنا أحط فلوسي هنا وأنا بقدر أحط في عقار؟" — Why put money here when I could invest in real estate?
- "غالي" — It's expensive

### Practical
- "أنا في السعودية — إزاي هتابع المشروع؟" — I'm in Saudi — how will I monitor the project?
- "ومين هيتابع الأرض لو مسافر؟" — Who manages the land if I'm traveling?
- "لو في مشكلة هحلها ازاي وأنا مش في مصر؟" — If there's a problem, how do I solve it from abroad?

## 4. Trust-Building Phrases (What Buyers Want to Hear)

### Process Clarity
- "هقول لك بالظبط هيحصل ايه" — I'll tell you exactly what will happen
- "كل خطوة موثقة" — Every step is documented
- "ادخل شوف بنفسك" — Come see for yourself
- "مفيش وعود فاضية — فيه عمليات حقيقية" — No empty promises — there are real operations

### Founder Trust
- "أنا اللي وراء الموضوع — أنا صاحب المشروع" — I'm behind this — I'm the project owner [from meeting transcript]
- "أنا بيع — اللي عارفني هيجي من الفيديوهات بتاعتي" — I'm a salesman — those who know me will come from my videos [from meeting]
- "اللي مش عارفني هيجي من المشكلة بتاعته" — Those who don't know me will come from their own problem [from meeting]
- "الموضوع ده على ضمانتي" — This is on my guarantee [from meeting]

### Verification
- "نرتبلك زيارة" — We'll arrange a visit for you
- "اتفضل شوف الأرض" — Come see the land
- "كل حاجة ليها تقارير" — Everything has reports
- "فيه timestamps وصور حقيقية" — There are timestamps and real photos

## 5. Phrases to AVOID (Anti-Language)

These phrases are common in competitor messaging but will trigger skepticism in Basseqat's audience:
- "ارباح مضمونة" — Guaranteed profits
- "عائد ٥٠٪ سنوياً" — 50% annual returns (compliance problem + credibility problem)
- "فرصة العمر" — Opportunity of a lifetime
- "استثمار بدون مخاطر" — Risk-free investment
- "اشتري دلوقتي قبل فوات الأوان" — Buy now before it's too late
- "حقق أحلامك" — Achieve your dreams
- "ثروة حقيقية" — Real wealth

## 6. Language Register Notes

### Recommended Register
- Egyptian colloquial Arabic (not MSA) for ads, WhatsApp, and landing page copy
- Serious but warm tone — not formal/stiff, not casual/comedic
- Short sentences, direct phrasing
- Specific details over abstract promises
- Real operations language (irrigation, cultivation, harvest, documentation) over financial jargon
- "أنا" / "إحنا" voice — personal, not corporate

### Register to Avoid
- MSA (Modern Standard Arabic) — sounds distant and institutional
- Gulf Arabic — audience is Egyptian
- Translated English marketing language — sounds fake
- Corporate-formal tone — sounds like a bank or institution, not a trusted guide
