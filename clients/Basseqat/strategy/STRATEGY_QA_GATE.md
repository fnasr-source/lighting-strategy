# Strategy QA Gate

Use this gate before a strategy is presented, approved, or turned into build tasks.

## Pass Conditions
- the full direct-response strategy playbook is completed section by section
- each major claim is either source-backed or clearly labeled as inference
- the main persona is specific enough to build the funnel around
- the offer logic is clear, commercially viable, and connected to proof
- the messaging is market-native and not generic or translated-sounding
- the funnel path is coherent across ads, landing page, WhatsApp, and follow-up
- measurement logic exists for leads, funnel, channel, and retention where relevant
- critical missing inputs are surfaced in `docs/client_kb/MISSING_INPUTS.md`
- contamination risks are reviewed in `docs/research/CONTAMINATION_CHECKLIST.md`

## Fail Conditions
- sections are skipped or hand-waved
- major claims have no evidence path
- the strategy reads like it could fit any client
- it relies on hype or assumptions without proof
- the main persona is too broad
- the offer or CTA is disconnected from the audience and market reality
- research depth is thin or superficial
- unresolved blockers still exist but are not clearly marked

## Required Output
Create or update:
- `docs/strategy/strategy-qa.md`

The output should state:
- approve / revise / reject
- key strengths
- key risks
- missing confirmations
- exact documents that need changes
