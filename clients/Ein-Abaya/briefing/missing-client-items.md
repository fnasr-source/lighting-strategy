# Ein Abaya Missing Client Items

This is the trimmed list of information still needed after checking the proposal, meeting transcripts, internal records, and the partner onboarding checklist.

## Needed To Start The Zid Build Well

- Confirm the daily approver for design/content decisions:
  name, email, and WhatsApp.
- Share Zid access details:
  store link, invite method, and any API constraints if relevant.
- Confirm who controls the domain and DNS.
- Confirm the active payment gateway and whether Tamara or Tabby are required.
- Confirm the current shipping setup:
  couriers, delivery SLAs, domestic vs international rules, COD if applicable.
- Share the exact accounts that can be granted access now:
  Meta, TikTok, Google Ads, GA4, GTM, and any other analytics or ad tools.
- Share final links or files for:
  shipping policy, refund policy, privacy policy, terms.
- Share the brand assets folder link:
  logos, colors, fonts, guidelines.
- Share the media folder link:
  hero videos, campaign imagery, product imagery, lifestyle imagery.
- Share the latest catalog export or product file for migration/reference.

## Needed To Make The New Site Sell Better

- Confirm current priority collections and best sellers.
- Confirm the exact welcome offer or evergreen promotion to highlight.
- Share reference competitor or inspiration sites.
- Share any review assets, customer feedback, or social-proof material that can be reused.
- Confirm the most important objections to answer on product pages:
  price, quality, fit, shipping, or something else.

## Intentionally Not Requested Yet

These are useful later, but not required to unblock kickoff:

- exact monthly budget ranges
- precise AOV / CPA / ROAS numbers
- full historical dashboard exports
- detailed campaign financials
- commercial pricing discussion already covered elsewhere
