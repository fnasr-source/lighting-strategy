# Partner Kickoff Checklist - Extracted Notes

Source reviewed: `Ein Abaya — Client Onboarding.pdf` provided by the user.

## Main categories in the PDF
- Technical and platform access
- Global technical access
- Payment gateways and BNPL
- CRM and customer-experience tools
- Legal and compliance
- Brand assets and identity
- Business and catalog information
- Marketing and conversion strategy
- Daily communication and approvals

## Questions / requests brought into the final combined onboarding

### Access and platform
- Zid manager access / permissions
- API details if needed
- Domain registrar / DNS ownership
- Meta, TikTok, Google, GA4, GTM access
- Payment gateways and BNPL
- Shipping / logistics setup

### Brand and assets
- Brand asset folder links
- Media folder links
- Logo / visual system readiness
- Reviews and social-proof assets

### Catalog and business rules
- Product collections / priority items
- Catalog export link
- Shipping rules / delivery logic
- Promotions / evergreen offers

### Strategy and conversion
- Brand positioning
- Target audience / personas
- Customer journey and objections
- Tone of voice
- Channels currently working / not working
- Competitor / reference brands

### Approvals
- Primary contact
- Daily approver

## What was intentionally not forced into the client-facing form
- Every single field from the partner PDF was **not** carried over one-to-one.
- The public form was simplified to reduce friction and speed up kickoff.
- Items that are useful but not essential for immediate Zid execution were either:
  - merged into broader questions,
  - marked optional,
  - or deferred to later operational follow-up.
