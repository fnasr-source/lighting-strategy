## Key Points

### Website rebuild priority
- Awatif made it clear that the website itself is the highest priority.
- Fouad explained that the work is not just visual branding; it is conversion-rate improvement through:
  - better product pages
  - stronger social proof
  - clearer trust messaging
  - more effective review loops
  - lifecycle email / retention logic

### Platform discussion at this stage
- The current store context referenced **Salla**.
- Shopify was recommended from a marketing / tracking / scaling standpoint.
- Fouad clarified that work on strategy and website could move in parallel.
- The client did not want current selling to stop while improvements were happening.

### Conversion and reporting
- Awatif asked how items such as abandoned carts and website behavior would be measured.
- Fouad described dashboards that bring together:
  - average order value
  - conversion rate
  - other store metrics
- The focus was on having one place where the full picture is visible.

### Outcome of the call
- The client asked for a website-focused proposal.
- Fouad confirmed that if the scope starts with website rebuild first, that can be separated and handled as the first step.
