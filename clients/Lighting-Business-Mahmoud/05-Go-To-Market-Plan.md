## Phase 1: Foundation & Validation (Weeks 1-4)
**Goal:** Prove the "Lighting Stylist" model works before building the full platform.

### Deliverables
*   **Brand Identity:** Name, logo, colors, tone of voice — positioned as the "Anti-Mousky" concierge.
*   **Quiz MVP:** A Typeform/Tally quiz ("What is your room style?") → leads to WhatsApp consultation.
*   **Content Starter Pack:** 10 Instagram posts (Before & After room transformations) + 3 TikTok/Reels.
*   **Supply Chain:** Close dropship/commission deals with Kandil + 2 quality importers on 50 hero items.
*   **Ad Pilot:** $500 Meta campaign (Egypt targeting). At CPM $0.59, this buys **~850,000 impressions**.

### KPIs
| Metric | Target |
| :--- | :--- |
| Quiz Completions | 200 |
| Consultations Booked | 50 |
| First Sales | 10 |
| Cost Per Consultation | < EGP 100 ($2) |

## Phase 2: The "Decision Engine" Build (Weeks 5-8)
**Goal:** Automate the recommendation process and create a defensible tech asset.

### Deliverables
*   **Platform:** Custom-built site (Next.js or Shopify Plus) with quiz integration.
*   **Key Feature:** "Room Visualizer" — upload photo → overlay chandelier → see it in context.
*   **Content Engine:** Film 10 "Review" videos (expert explaining *why* this crystal is better than that one).
*   **Payment Integration:** Sympl + ValU installments, credit card, cash on delivery.
*   **"Safe Arrival Guarantee"** program launched (insurance built into pricing).
*   **SEO:** Target "نجفة لريسبشن" (chandelier for reception), "إضاءة فيلا" (villa lighting) + long-tail.

### KPIs
| Metric | Target |
| :--- | :--- |
| Online Orders | 100 |
| Average Order Value | > EGP 5,000 |
| Return Rate | < 5% (thanks to visualizer) |
| Instagram Followers | 5,000 |
