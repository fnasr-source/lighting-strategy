## Filters To Prefer
- business email only
- verified email only
- current role
- institution domain present
- one lead per institution when possible for the first pass
- organizations whose names clearly indicate a university, college, institute, academy, faculty, school, or higher-education entity

## Filters To Avoid
- students
- interns
- alumni relations
- admissions-only roles
- generic IT support without education responsibility
- hobbyist or community-only contacts

## What Good Leads Look Like
- university or training institution email
- practical ownership of labs, training, curriculum, or cyber programs
- ability to influence one cohort pilot
- clear institutional context on LinkedIn or company profile

## What Bad Leads Look Like
- no obvious connection to education delivery
- too junior to influence anything
- general IT titles with no training or academic context
- non-institutional free email domains
